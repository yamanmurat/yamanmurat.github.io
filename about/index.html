About Me

I am a dedicated professional with a comprehensive understanding of quantum mechanics and molecular dynamics simulations, particularly focused on unraveling the behavior of small molecules in complex systems.

My expertise lies in leveraging computational techniques to gain deep insights into molecular interactions and dynamics. I am also adept at utilizing Python for data analysis, employing it extensively in my work to process and extract meaningful insights from simulation results. Furthermore, I am actively contributing to the development of an in-house carbohydrate parser program, harnessing my coding skills to enhance its functionality. Additionally, I have a strong passion for exploring the realms of machine learning and drug discovery.

I am currently engaged in intensive learning and development in these fields, driven by an insatiable curiosity and a desire to apply these cutting-edge technologies to advance scientific research. With a solid background in quantum mechanics, molecular dynamics, Python coding, and data analysis, coupled with a burgeoning interest in machine learning and drug discovery, I am poised to contribute to the exciting intersection of computational chemistry and innovative research in the future.
